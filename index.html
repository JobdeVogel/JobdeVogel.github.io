Grasshopper

In the last five years I spent many hours in Grasshopper. I have developed several projects and tools within Grasshopper, using the visual nodes, or standard Python programming. Furthermore, I have tutored and lectured Grasshopper to bachelor students and developed a new GH study program for TU Delft. You can find this study program in the link down below. I am familiar with most functions and the background principles of Grasshopper. Since it is not possible to work in Grasshopper all the time, I try to stay up-to-date through the McNeel forum. In the future I hope to research alternative parametric modelling software like Maya or Unity as alternative to Grasshopper, specifically to be able to handle and process more data than possible in Grasshopper.
